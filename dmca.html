DMCA / Copyright Policy

Respect for Intellectual Property

PlayZen respects the intellectual property rights of game developers, publishers, and all content creators. All games featured on this Site are embedded from their original, third-party sources. PlayZen does not host, store, or redistribute game files. Each game remains the property of its respective owner.

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you are a copyright owner (or authorized to act on behalf of one) and believe that content accessible through PlayZen infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown notice. We will review all valid notices promptly and take appropriate action, which may include removing the embed link from the Site.

What to Include in a Notice

To help us process your request efficiently, please include the following information:

  1. A description of the copyrighted work you believe is being infringed.
  2. The specific URL(s) on PlayZen where the allegedly infringing content can be found.
  3. Your full legal name, mailing address, phone number, and email address.
  4. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the owner's behalf.
  6. Your physical or electronic signature.

How to Submit a Notice

You can submit a copyright notice by:

  • Email: dmca@playzen.live
  • Using our Contact page with "Copyright / DMCA" selected as the reason.

Our Response

Upon receiving a valid takedown notice, we will:

  • Review the claim promptly.
  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing embed if the notice is valid.
  • Make reasonable efforts to notify the affected party, if applicable.

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed or disabled by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notification with the following:

  1. Identification of the material that was removed and the URL where it appeared.
  2. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the material was removed by mistake.
  3. Your name, address, phone number, and email address.
  4. A statement consenting to the jurisdiction of your local federal court.
  5. Your physical or electronic signature.

Repeat Infringers

PlayZen reserves the right to remove any content and terminate access for repeat infringers at our discretion.
