Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PlayZen ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website located at playzen.live (the "Site"). This is a practical starter policy for a small, ad-supported static website. It is not legal advice. We recommend consulting a licensed attorney before launching.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, our hosting provider and analytics tools may automatically collect standard server log data, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps. This data helps us understand traffic patterns and improve the Site.

1.2 Cookies

We may use first-party and third-party cookies for analytics and advertising. Third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense) may set their own cookies to serve personalized or contextual ads. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

1.3 localStorage

We use your browser's localStorage to store preferences such as favorite games, recently played games, and continue-playing data. This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics tools (e.g., Plausible Analytics, Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with the Site. These tools may collect anonymized usage data such as page views, session duration, and referral sources.

3. Advertising Partners

The Site may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks, including Google AdSense. These partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your browsing activity. For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's partner site policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info/choices or optout.networkadvertising.org.

4. Embedded Third-Party Content

The Site embeds games and content from third-party sources via iframes. These third-party sites may independently collect data, set cookies, or track interactions according to their own privacy policies. PlayZen does not control the privacy practices of these third-party providers.

5. Your Choices

  • Cookies: You can disable or clear cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • localStorage: You can clear your browser's local storage to remove saved preferences (favorites, recently played, etc.).
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads using the links above.
  • Do Not Track: We respect Do Not Track signals where technically feasible.

6. Children's Privacy

PlayZen is a general-audience website. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information collected through the Site. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@playzen.live or through our Contact page.
