What Is Dots and Boxes?

The classic pen-and-paper strategy game. Take turns drawing lines between dots. When you complete the fourth side of a box, you score a point and go again. The player with the most boxes wins.

Dots and Boxes runs entirely in your browser with no downloads or signups required. It works on desktop and mobile devices. Whether you have five minutes or an hour, Dots and Boxes is a perfect way to relax and challenge yourself.

Tips & Strategy

  • Avoid completing the third side of a box — it hands a point to your opponent.
  • Force your opponent to open up chains of boxes you can capture.
  • Control the endgame by managing how chains are opened.
  • Sacrifice a small chain early to gain control of larger chains later.
